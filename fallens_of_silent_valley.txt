1 Gold Unit:

Haunter (Strength: 1)
    Resolution: Discard an other friendly unit in combat to add 2 strength to this unit.


2 Gold Unit:

Faceless (Strength: 2)
    If this unit is discarded in combat before or during the Resolution step, you may draw a card immediately after resolving all other ongoing abilities.


3 Gold Units:

Poltergeist (Strength: 3)
    When played: Discard a friendly unit in combat to destroy an opposing unit. (There must be an opposing unit to trigger this ability.)

Lost soul (Strength: 3)
    When played: Choose a player. The player draw a card, then discard a card at random.


5 Gold Unit:

Wraith (Strength: 4)
    When played: Discard any unit in combat (except a Forest Guardian). If you discarded a friendly unit, look at the top 3 cards of your deck. Then discard any number of these cards and put back the rest on top of your deck in any order.


6 Gold Legendary Unit:

Revenants Army (Strength: 3)
    Every time a card is discarded during your turn, you can instead exclude it from play. When this unit is discarded or destroyed, return all cards excluded by this unit to the discard pile of their respective owners.
    Resolution: Add 1 strength to this unit per card excluded by this unit.
