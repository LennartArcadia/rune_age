1 Gold Unit: 

Unskilled Apprentice (Strength: 1)
    Destroy this unit at the beginning of the resolution step if it is wounded.
    When played : You may wound or heal 1 friendly unit.


2 Gold Unit: 

Master of Poisons (Strength: 2)
    When played: You may wound 2 friendly units. If you do so, you may destroy 1 participating unit (friendly or opposing).


3 Gold Units:

Forbidden Adept (Strength: 2)
    If a friendly unit should be destroyed, you may wound two friendly units instead. 

Expensive Patrician (Strength: 3)
    When played: You may discard any number of Gold cards. If you do, you may heal as many units as their combined value and draw a card. If at least two units were healed that way, you may draw an additional card.


5 Gold unit: 

Experimental Creation (Strength: 4)
    When the experimental creation is healed, put a marker on it.
    Resolution: add 1 strength to this unit for each marker on it.


6 Gold Legendary Unit: 

God Appearance (Strength: 2)
    When played: You may heal any number of units on the battlefield.
    Resolution: add 1 strength to each unwounded friendly unit.
